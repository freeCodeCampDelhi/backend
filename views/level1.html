Introduction

Ever heard the phrase “Work smart, not hard”? Well computers don’t do that at all. It’s best to think of a computer as a really dumb but hard working human being. It can do great things with good guidance, like running Facebook. But without good guidance, it’s just a hunk of metal. That’s where you come in, with your guidance and support you can make a computer do great things. You can make computers great again. :)

Before we go any further and make cool stuff like the next Google, lets figure out how to talk to these computers. We talk to computers through something called Programming and there are many Programming languages to choose from, for the purpose of this course we’ll choose the most popular language, Python! :D

Introduction to Python Syntax

If programming is the act of teaching a computer to have a conversation with a user, it would be most useful to first teach the computer how to speak. In Python, this is accomplished with the print statement.

print "Hello, world!"
print "I'm a programmer at The Girl Code! :)"

A print statement is the easiest way to get your Python program to communicate with you. Being able to command this communication will be one of the most valuable tools in your programming toolbox.
