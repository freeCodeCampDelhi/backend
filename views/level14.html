<
 After taking an input from the user, you've to make all possible cases for the possible outcomes. These cases are:
1. The computer and player's move is the same- this will produce a tie
2. The player is scissor and the computer is paper- the player wins.
3. The player is rock and the computer is scissor- the player wins.
4. The player is paper and the computer is scissor- the player loses.
5. The player is rock and the computer is paper- the player loses.
